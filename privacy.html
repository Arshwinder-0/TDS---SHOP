Our Privacy Promise

At The Diamond Store, we believe your personal data belongs to you. We collect only what's necessary, never sell your information, and always protect your privacy.

Information We Collect
What personal data we gather from you

We collect minimal information necessary to process your orders and provide excellent service. Here's exactly what we collect:

Information Required? Purpose
Your Name Optional To personalize communication
WhatsApp Number Required To deliver your subscription & provide support
Instagram Username If ordering via IG To communicate if you order via Instagram
Transaction ID For verification To verify payment (not stored permanently)
What We DON'T Collect
We do NOT collect your email address, physical address, location data, bank details, or any other sensitive information. Transaction IDs are used for verification only and not permanently stored.

How we collect this information:

WhatsApp Chat Instagram DM Website Contact
No Automatic Data Collection
Our website does NOT automatically collect any data about you. We don't use analytics, tracking pixels, or any form of automatic data collection.
Why We Collect Your Data
The purpose behind our data collection

We only collect data for one simple reason: to process your orders and deliver your subscriptions.

  • Order Processing: Your WhatsApp number is needed to send you subscription credentials
  • Payment Verification: Transaction ID helps us confirm your payment
  • Customer Support: To help you if any issues arise with your subscription
  • Order Communication: To update you about your order status
About Marketing & Promotions
We post updates and offers on our Instagram page (@tdsofficial.store) for everyone to see. We do NOT send promotional DMs or marketing messages to your WhatsApp. You'll only receive messages related to your orders.
How We Protect Your Data
Security measures we take

We take your privacy seriously and implement multiple security measures to protect your information.

Our Security Measures
Your data is stored securely on password and biometric-protected personal devices. We never share our devices with others.
  • Device Protection: Password and biometric (fingerprint/face) lock on all devices
  • Personal Storage Only: Data stored on personal device, not shared storage
  • Limited Access: Only business owner has access to customer data
  • No Third-Party Storage: We don't store your data on external servers
  • Regular Cleanup: Old data is deleted after subscription period ends

Data Retention:

How Long We Keep Your Data
We only keep your information during your active subscription period. Once your subscription ends and there are no pending issues, your data is deleted from our records.
Data Sharing & Third Parties
Who can access your information
We NEVER Share or Sell Your Data
Your personal information stays with us. We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data to any third party for any reason.

Third-Party Services We Use:

While we don't share your data, we do use these platforms to communicate with you:

Service Purpose Data Shared
WhatsApp Order communication & delivery Your messages to us
Instagram Alternative communication Your DMs to us
Paytm/UPI Payment processing Transaction happens on their platform
Note About These Platforms
WhatsApp, Instagram, and payment apps have their own privacy policies. When you use these services, their privacy policies apply to the data you share on their platforms.
Cookies & Tracking
Our cookie and tracking policy
No Cookies, No Tracking
Our website does NOT use cookies. We do NOT track your browsing behavior, page views, or any other online activities.
  • No Cookies: We don't store any cookies on your browser
  • No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics or similar tools
  • No Tracking Pixels: We don't track your activity across websites
  • No Fingerprinting: We don't collect device fingerprint data

Your visit to our website is completely private and anonymous. We have no way of knowing who visits our site or what pages they view.

Your Privacy Rights
What you can request from us

You have full control over your personal data. Here are your rights:

Right to Access
Ask us what data we have about you anytime
Right to Deletion
Request us to delete all your data
Right to Correction
Ask us to correct any wrong information
Right to Know
Ask how your data is being used
How to Exercise Your Rights
Simply message us on WhatsApp or Instagram and tell us what you need. We'll respond within 24-48 hours and take action accordingly.

No Marketing Opt-Out Needed:

Since we don't send promotional messages or marketing DMs, there's no need for an opt-out option. You'll only receive messages related to your orders.

Children's Privacy
Age requirements for our services
Age Requirement: 13+
Our services are available to users who are 13 years of age or older. Users under 18 should have parent/guardian consent.
  • Minimum Age: You must be at least 13 years old to use our services
  • Parental Consent: Users between 13-18 should have parental permission
  • Payment: Since payments require UPI/bank access, minors should purchase through parents

If we discover that we've collected data from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Policy
How we update this privacy policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons.

  • Updates: Changes will be posted on this page
  • Date: The "Last Updated" date will be changed
  • Major Changes: For significant changes, we may post on Instagram
  • Continued Use: Using our services after changes means you accept the new policy
Stay Updated
Follow our Instagram page @tdsofficial.store to stay informed about any important updates to our policies or services.
